Under the guise that sometimes desperate times require desperate measures, and if you would rather not drop the tank, my approach would be to use a hammer and chisel. I assume the bushing is rubber which could easily be chiseled through to reach the screw. Before you chisel be sure to place a piece of wood between the bolt and the body pan in can use slip…

If you can get access by dropping the tank that may be the least effort and most productive. It gives you the opportunity to clean the tank, replace the sending unit and the sending unit seal if they’ve never been replaced.
