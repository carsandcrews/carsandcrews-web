# Automotive Enthusiast Site — Overview

**Where car culture comes together.**

## What It Is

A unified platform for automotive enthusiasts. The place to discover car events happening near you, showcase your vehicles, and connect with the people who share your passion — all in one site.

Three pillars:
- **Events** — Find car shows, Cars & Coffee meets, cruise-ins, swap meets, and rallies. Search by location, date, or type. RSVP and let people know what you're bringing.
- **Garage** — Create profiles for your vehicles with photos, specs, and the story behind each build. Your garage is your portfolio.
- **Community** — Follow builders, meet local enthusiasts, and find your crew. Whether it's a local club or a loose group of friends who show up every Saturday, this is where you connect.

## Who It's For

The car culture lifestyle crowd — not just owners, but everyone who shows up:
- People who attend shows, meets, and cruises
- Builders and restorers who want to showcase their work
- Photographers and spectators who love the scene
- Event organizers who need a better way to reach their audience
- Club leaders looking for a modern home for their crew

The audience skews GenX and younger — digitally fluent, visually driven, and tired of hunting through Facebook groups and scattered event listings to find what's happening this weekend.

## What Makes It Different

- **One place, not five.** Events, vehicles, and community live together. Your RSVP shows which car you're bringing. Your garage links to the events you've attended. Everything connects.
- **Events you can actually find.** We crawl the web, curate listings, and let anyone submit events they know about. Organizers can claim their events and manage them directly.
- **Built for car people, by car people.** Not a general-purpose social network with a car group bolted on. Every feature is designed for how this community actually works.
- **Photos first.** The site is built around your photos — phone quality, mixed lighting, parking lot backgrounds and all. We make them look good.

## Business Model

Free to use. Premium features for power users and event organizers come later — only after the free experience proves its value.
