A: Media-Rich Hero + Utility Below RECOMMENDED

Big, cinematic featured event hero that sets the mood. Scroll down and you immediately hit utility — nearby events, quick stats, search. Emotional hook up top, practical value underneath.

Pros

  • Visual impact draws people in
  • Featured events drive engagement
  • Feels premium and curated

Cons

  • Requires good hero content from day one
  • Utility is below the fold

B: Utility-First with Search

Search bar and event type filters front and center. "What's happening near you?" is the immediate question answered. Event cards appear right away. Functional, fast, Google-Maps-meets-Eventbrite energy.

Pros

  • Instant utility — no scrolling needed
  • Works even with minimal content
  • Clear value proposition

Cons

  • Less emotional impact
  • Can feel generic/utilitarian

C: Hybrid — Visual Strip + Unified Search

Compact visual banner (not full-screen hero) with featured/trending/nearby, then immediately into a unified search bar and mixed-content feed (events, vehicles, people). Best of both — visual personality without burying utility.

Pros

  • Visual hook without hiding utility
  • Unified search across all content types
  • Scales well as content grows

Cons

  • Slightly busier than the other two
  • More to design/build upfront
